🎫 Tickets and Pricing

Current day-entry ticket prices (inclusive of 18% GST):

  • Water Park Adult: ₹1,299 per person
  • Amusement Park Adult: ₹1,199 per person
  • Combo (Both Parks): ₹1,999 per person — Save ₹499!
  • WOW Passport (Annual): ₹4,999 per year

Child concessions apply for heights 90–130 cm. Infants below 90 cm / 3 yrs enter FREE.

Yes! Group discounts are available:
  • 4 to 9 guests: ₹75 off per ticket
  • 10 to 24 guests: ₹125 off per ticket
  • 25+ guests: Contact us for custom pricing at groups@worldsofwonder.in
Was this helpful?
Yes. Special concession rates apply for: Senior Citizens (60+ years), Armed Forces personnel (with valid ID), and Differently Abled visitors. Present a valid government-issued ID at the booking counter for these rates. Contact our helpline for current concession pricing.
Was this helpful?
Tickets are available both at the park gate and online. However, online booking is strongly recommended as: (1) peak weekends sell out, (2) you get instant QR delivery, (3) no booking fee, (4) you skip the queue at the gate. Book at worldsofwonder.in/book.

🕙 Timings and Entry
The park is open daily from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Last entry is at 4:00 PM. On Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays, extended hours apply (9:00 AM to 7:00 PM). Call 080-6909-0000 to confirm holiday hours.
Was this helpful?
Yes, the park is open 365 days a year including all public holidays. In the event of heavy rain or severe weather advisory, specific water rides may be closed temporarily for safety. The park itself remains open. Guests are advised to check our website or call ahead during monsoon season.
Was this helpful?
Day-entry ticket holders are not permitted to re-enter after exiting the park. WOW Passport holders may exit and re-enter on the same day by showing their passport QR. If you need to exit briefly for an emergency, speak to the gate staff before leaving.
Was this helpful?
🛡 Safety and Rules
The following are strictly prohibited: outside food and beverages, alcoholic drinks, glass containers, sharp objects, drones, professional cameras (DSLR), pets, and fireworks. Bags are subject to security screening at the entrance.
Was this helpful?
Yes, most thrill rides have minimum height requirements for safety. Common restrictions: Roller Coasters and Speed Slides — minimum 120 cm. Family rides — minimum 90 cm or accompanied by adult. Please check individual ride boards at the park. Children below the minimum height will not be permitted on those rides.
Was this helpful?
Yes. A fully equipped First Aid Centre is located near the main entrance and at two additional points within the park. Trained medical staff are on duty during all park hours. For emergencies, call the Park Control Room at 0120-456-7890.
Was this helpful?
⭐ WOW Passport
WOW Passport is the annual unlimited membership that gives you unrestricted access to both the Water Park and the Amusement Park for 365 days. Pricing: Individual ₹4,999, Couple ₹8,999, Family (2+2) ₹15,999. It pays for itself in just 4 visits!
Was this helpful?
The WOW Passport covers standard entry to all rides in both parks. Certain premium experiences such as special VIP event tickets, F&B, lockers, and merchandise are charged separately. However, passport holders get 10-20% discount on F&B and 15% on merchandise.
Was this helpful?
👥 Groups and Events
Use our online Group Booking form or call 080-6909-0000 for groups of 10 or more. We offer bulk pricing, coach parking, dedicated entry lanes, custom catering, and on-ground coordination for schools, colleges, and corporate teams.
Was this helpful?
Absolutely! WOW offers dedicated birthday party packages starting from ₹499 per guest (minimum 10 guests). Packages include decorated seating area, cake-cutting zone, F&B vouchers, and a special birthday announcement. Contact our events team at events@worldsofwonder.in.
Was this helpful?
🏟 Park Facilities
Yes. Separate changing rooms for men and women are available near the Water Park entrance. Lockers are available for a ₹10 refundable coin deposit. You will receive the coin back when you return the key.
Was this helpful?
Yes, paid parking is available on-site for cars, bikes, and buses. Charges are applicable. There is also a dedicated drop-off/pick-up zone at the main entrance for cabs and autos. On weekends, a free shuttle service runs from Noida City Centre Metro Station.
Was this helpful?
WOW is committed to being an inclusive experience. Wheelchair ramps are available at key entry and exit points. Accessible restrooms are available at multiple locations. Certain ride platforms are accessible. Differently abled guests also receive a concession on ticket price with valid ID.
Was this helpful?
🍔 Food and Dining
No. Outside food and beverages are not permitted inside the park. However, multiple food courts, casual dining, snack kiosks, and beverage counters are available inside with a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Baby food and infant formula are permitted.
Was this helpful?
Yes. The food courts offer a wide selection of vegetarian dishes. Jain and specific dietary requirements can be accommodated at the main food court — please inform the staff. For severe allergies, it is advisable to consult with park F&B management before visiting.
Was this helpful?
🚗 Getting Here
Worlds of Wonder is located at A-2, Sector 38A, Noida, Uttar Pradesh 201301. It is approximately 15 minutes from Noida City Centre Metro Station (Blue Line). Open in Google Maps.
Was this helpful?
Yes. A free shuttle service is available on Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays from Noida City Centre Metro Station. The shuttle runs every 30 minutes from 9:00 AM. No pre-booking is required — just show your valid park ticket to board.
Was this helpful?
↩ Refunds and Changes
Day-entry tickets are generally non-refundable. However, if the park closes due to operational reasons or severe weather on your visit date, a full credit valid for 60 days will be issued. One reschedule is permitted with 24 hours notice. For assistance, call 080-6909-0000.
Was this helpful?
Yes, one free date change is allowed per booking if requested at least 24 hours before the original visit date. Call our helpline at 080-6909-0000 or email support@worldsofwonder.in with your booking ID and preferred new date.
