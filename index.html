Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

The park is open daily from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Last entry is at 4:00 PM. Extended hours apply on weekends and public holidays (9:00 AM to 7:00 PM).
The Combo ticket (₹1,999/person) includes unrestricted same-day access to both the Water Park (20+ rides including Wave Pool, Lazy River, Slides) and the Amusement Park (30+ rides including Rockin Roller coaster, Giant Wheel, Live Shows). It saves ₹499 versus buying both tickets separately.
Children below 90 cm height (generally under 3 years) enter FREE. Children between 90 cm and 130 cm are eligible for child ticket rates. Please check current pricing on the booking page.
We are located at A-2, Sector 38A, Noida, UP 201301. Take the Blue Line Metro to Noida City Centre, then a 15-minute auto/cab ride. By road, take NH-24/NH-9. Free shuttle service runs on weekends from Noida City Centre Metro Station.
Day-entry tickets are non-refundable. One free date-change is permitted with 24 hours notice. If the park closes due to severe weather on your visit day, a full credit valid for 60 days will be issued. Call 080-6909-0000 for assistance.
Outside food and beverages are not permitted. Multiple food courts and dining options are available inside the park. Infant food and formula are allowed.
