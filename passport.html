How to Get Your WOW Passport

The entire process is digital. Takes less than 5 minutes from your phone.

1

Choose Your Plan and Pay Online

Select Individual, Couple, or Family plan. Pay securely via UPI, Card, Net Banking, or Wallet. Instant confirmation.

2

Complete Digital KYC

Upload a selfie and Aadhaar/PAN for quick identity verification. Automated OCR reads your details. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Verification and Activation (24-48 hours)

Our team verifies your KYC. You receive an SMS and WhatsApp confirmation once your passport is activated.

4

Receive Your Digital Passport

Your unique QR code and passport ID arrive via WhatsApp and Email. Valid for 365 days from activation date.

5

Visit Anytime — Show and Scan

Show your digital passport at the dedicated passport lane. Scan and walk straight in. No queuing.
