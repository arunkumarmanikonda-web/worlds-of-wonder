📋 What to Do at the Park

1

Show Your QR at the Entrance Gate

Keep this page open or download the ticket. The QR code is your entry pass. Staff will scan it at the turnstile.

2

Carry a Valid Photo ID

Aadhaar, Passport, Driving Licence or any government-issued ID must be presented for age-concession tickets.

3

Water Park: Bring Swimwear

Swimwear or park-issued costumes are required. Changing rooms and lockers (₹10 refundable coin) are available on-site.

4

Arrive by 10:00 AM for Full Day Fun

Park is open 10 AM to 6 PM daily. Last entry is at 4:00 PM. Weekends get busy — arrive early to avoid queues!
