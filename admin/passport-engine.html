🔐 Voucher QR Rules

Non-reusable, non-transferable. Each voucher linked to passport ID. Scanned once = redeemed forever.
RuleConfigurationApplies To
QR Generation Generated at passport issuance. Format: WOW-{TIER}-{PASSPORT_ID}-{VOUCHER_TYPE}-{SEQ} All tiers
Single-use lock On first scan → is_redeemed = true + timestamp stored. Repeat scan returns ❌ INVALID. All vouchers
Cross-validation Voucher passport_id must match the passport being used at the gate on the same visit. All tiers
Expiry check Voucher expires with passport expiry_date. Expired passports cannot redeem any vouchers. All tiers
Non-transferable Voucher is tied to holder biometric / passport photo. Agent must verify identity on-site. All tiers
Dispute audit trail Every redemption stored in passport_redemptions table with agent ID, device ID, timestamp, IP. All tiers

⚠️ Upsell Constraints (Hard Rules)

These cannot be overridden without CTO + Business Head approval
ConstraintDescriptionStatus
No AP/WP Split Parks must be offered as combined package only. No separate Amusement-only or Water-only passports. LOCKED
No Micro-Benefits Benefits valued at ₹10 or below (e.g., a single wafer voucher) are prohibited in any tier. LOCKED
Legacy Non-Dilution Legacy tier price, visit count, and premium perks (VIP lane, guest passes, costumes) cannot be reduced or matched by lower tiers. LOCKED
No Cross-Tier Sharing Passport vouchers cannot be shared between different passport holders or transferred to another user. LOCKED
