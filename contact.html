Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reschedule my visit date? +
Dated tickets are currently date-specific. Contact us at least 48 hours before your visit and our team will review rescheduling on a case-by-case basis.
How do I use my QR ticket at the gate? +
Show the QR code on your phone at the park gate scanner. It reads in under 2 seconds. Bring a valid photo ID matching your booking name.
How do group discounts work? +
Book 4+ tickets in a single booking and the group discount (₹50–₹125 per ticket) is automatically applied. No promo code needed.
What happens during a weather-related closure? +
If the park closes due to weather, affected tickets are automatically rescheduled or fully refunded. You'll receive an SMS and email notification.
What documents do I need for the Passport? +
KYC requires Aadhaar number and a selfie for digital identity verification. The process is fully online — no physical documents needed.
Are there height restrictions on rides? +
Some thrill rides require a minimum height of 120cm. Family rides and kids' zones are open to all ages. Height charts are displayed at each ride entrance.
