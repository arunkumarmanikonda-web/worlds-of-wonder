Every Drop of Fun

15+ water rides for every mood and every age — from total relaxation to total terror (the fun kind).

🌊
Wave Pool
Delhi NCR's largest wave pool — 2,500 sq m of churning water with multiple wave modes from gentle ripples to massive 4-foot surf swells. Ring floats available on-site.
Family Relaxing
🌀
Boomerango
WOW's iconic signature ride — a giant enclosed funnel bowl. Ride a tube at speed into a massive circular bowl, swing back and forth defying gravity, then shoot out the bottom into a splash pool.
Extreme Thrill Min. height: 120 cm
🛶
Lazy River
A 300-metre scenic float circuit around the park. Grab a ring tube, lie back completely, and let the current carry you. The most civilised ride in the park.
Relaxing Family
💃
Rain Disco
Multi-sensory rain dance arena with 1,000+ water jets, colourful mood lighting, a DJ booth and non-stop dance energy. The social heart of the water park — open daily.
Family Party Favourite
🚀
Speed Slides
Near-vertical open speed slides with a steep gradient and dramatic height drop. You will arrive at the bottom significantly faster than expected. You will immediately queue again.
Thrill Min. height: 120 cm
🛟
Tube Slides
Single and double enclosed tube slides with twists, turns and complete darkness sections. Ride solo for a solo scream, or double-up with a friend for a shared one.
Thrill Family
🏄
Body Slides & Mat Racer
Five open body slide lanes plus multi-lane mat racing tracks. Go head-to-head with your group down parallel lanes. Bragging rights to the winner.
Thrill Family
🧒
Kids Splash Zone
Dedicated children's area with shallow wading pools (max depth 60 cm), gentle toddler slides, interactive spray fountains and water cannons. For ages 2–10.
Ages 2–10 Safe Depth
🏊
Adult Swimming Pool
A dedicated 25-metre adult pool for guests who prefer genuine swimming to being hurled down a slide. Lap lanes available. Dignity fully preserved.
Relaxing Adults
🎪
Multi-Activity Pool
In-water volleyball, basketball hoops and pool games. Competitive families will love it. Non-competitive families will spectate and give unsolicited advice.
Family Fun Games
🌪
Tornado Slide
A massive open funnel bowl — different from the Boomerango. Riders swoosh around the inside of a giant open cone before dropping through the centre hole. Spectacular.
Thrill Family
⚡
Water Roller Coaster
A seated water coaster that goes both downhill and uphill using water jets — the only coaster experience that ends with a guaranteed soaking. Unique in Delhi NCR.
