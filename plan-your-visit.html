Getting Here

How to Reach Worlds of Wonder

🚗

By Car

Take NH-24 or NH-9 towards Noida. Follow signs to Sector 38A. The park is clearly signposted from the DND Flyway. GPS: "Worlds of Wonder Noida".

~25 min from Connaught Place
🚇

By Metro

Take Blue Line to Noida Sector 18. Auto-rickshaws and e-rickshaws available directly outside the metro station. 10–15 minute ride.

~40 min from Rajiv Chowk
🚌

By Bus

DTC and UP Roadways buses to Noida bus stand, then local transport to Sector 38A. Weekend shuttle service available from select metro stations.

Check weekend shuttle schedule
🛺

By Auto / Cab

Ola and Uber available throughout Delhi-NCR. Share the location "Worlds of Wonder, Sector 38A Noida" for precision drop-off at the main gate.

Book in advance on weekends
What to Carry

Come Prepared, Leave Happy

✅ Bring These

  • Your e-ticket QR code (on phone or printed)
  • Valid government photo ID (for concession categories)
  • Swimwear / swimming costume (Water Park)
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Waterproof footwear (flip-flops / crocs)
  • Change of clothes and towel
  • Light snacks — but F&B is available inside
  • Cash or UPI for inside purchases

❌ Leave These Behind

  • Outside food and beverages (not permitted)
  • Sharp objects, weapons
  • Glass bottles or containers
  • Drones or professional cameras
  • Alcohol or substances
  • Pets (not permitted in park)
  • Valuables you can't lock away
Admission Rules

Height, Age & Admission Guidelines

👶

Children Under 90 cm / 3 Years

Enter FREE — no ticket required. Must be accompanied by a paying adult at all times.

📏

Ride Height Restrictions

Certain thrill rides have minimum height requirements (typically 120–140 cm). Check individual ride signage at entry.

🩺

Health Advisories

Guests with heart conditions, epilepsy, pregnancy, or recent surgery are advised to consult their doctor before riding. Some rides carry advisory notices.

👗

Water Park Dress Code

Proper swimwear required for water rides — shorts, t-shirts, or jeans are not permitted on water slides. Swimwear rental available at ₹150.

Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Can I re-enter the park after going out?
Re-entry is not permitted once you exit. Plan your day to stay inside. Lockers are available to store belongings.
Is there a locker facility?
Yes. Lockers are available at both parks at ₹100–₹200 per day depending on size. Deposit refunded on return.
Are outside food and drinks allowed?
Outside food is not permitted inside the park. Multiple food courts, snack kiosks, and restaurants are available inside with a wide range of options.
What if it rains?
The Water Park stays open in light rain — it's a water park, after all! In case of a thunderstorm, the park follows safety protocols and rides may be temporarily halted. No refunds for weather-related delays.
Is the park wheelchair accessible?
The park has wheelchair-friendly pathways in public areas. Differently-Abled guests receive a 50% concession on tickets. Please call ahead so we can arrange assistance.
Can I book on the day itself?
Yes, but we strongly recommend booking online in advance. Weekend walk-in queues can be long, and some dates sell out. Online booking also lets you skip the queue entirely.
What is the cancellation/refund policy?
Tickets cancelled 24+ hours before the visit date are eligible for a 90% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. Contact [email protected].
Is there first aid available?
Yes. A fully equipped first-aid centre with trained paramedics is stationed inside both parks 10 AM–6 PM daily.
