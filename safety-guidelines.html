🛡 Safety Guidelines

Your Safety is Our
First Priority. Always.

Please read these guidelines before your visit. Understanding the rules protects you, your family, and every guest at Worlds of Wonder.

General Rules

Park-Wide Safety Rules

These rules apply across both the Water Park and Amusement Park.

01

Follow All Instructions

Always obey instructions from WOW staff, ride operators, and lifeguards. Their instructions override any personal judgement.

02

No Running in Wet Areas

Running on wet surfaces, pool decks, and water ride platforms is strictly prohibited. Wet floors cause falls — walk carefully at all times.

03

No Diving or Jumping

Diving headfirst into pools, jumping off platforms, or entering water in ways not designed for the attraction is dangerous and forbidden.

04

Respect Queues

Queue-jumping, pushing, or aggressive behaviour at ride entry points will result in removal from the attraction without refund.

05

Secure Loose Items

Remove glasses, jewellery, footwear, and loose items before boarding rides. Items lost during rides cannot be recovered. Use lockers.

06

No Alcohol or Substances

Guests who appear intoxicated will be refused entry to rides and may be asked to leave the park for the safety of all guests.

⚕ Health Advisories

Please Do Not Ride If You Have…

Certain health conditions make thrill rides risky. Please consult your doctor before visiting if any of the below apply.

❤️Heart conditions or high blood pressure
🤰Pregnancy (any stage)
🧠Epilepsy or seizure disorders
🦴Recent surgery or bone fractures
🩹Back, neck or spinal conditions
😵Vertigo or motion sickness (for high rides)
🩺Severe asthma or respiratory conditions
💊Currently on medication affecting balance
Note: Even on family-grade rides, forces and movements may be unexpected. If in doubt, sit out. No one will judge. There are plenty of attractions — and a Wave Pool — for everyone.
🌊 Water Park Safety

Water Park Rules

    ✅ You Must

  • Wear appropriate swimwear on all water rides
  • Use life vests if you cannot swim (available free at entry)
  • Follow lifeguard instructions at all times in pool areas
  • Enter slides feet-first in the position indicated at the top
  • Wait for the signal from the operator before entering a slide
  • Exit the splash pool quickly after dismounting
  • Keep children within arm's reach in the wave pool

    ❌ You Must Not

  • Enter the wave pool if you cannot swim without a life vest
  • Wear street clothes (jeans, t-shirts) on water slides
  • Use the slides if you have open cuts or wounds
  • Bring glass containers anywhere near the pool areas
  • Allow children under 90 cm in deep wave pool areas unaccompanied
  • Push others at the top of slides or in the splash pools
  • Block the flow on slides by stopping mid-slide

🚨 Emergency

Emergency Numbers & Procedures

🏥

First Aid Centre

Located at the main entrance of both parks. Staffed by trained paramedics 10 AM – 6 PM daily. For minor injuries, bites, allergic reactions.

Park Helpline: 080-6909-0000
🛟

Lifeguard Stations

Trained lifeguards are stationed at all pool areas, wave pools, and slide splash zones. In case of emergency in the water, signal a lifeguard immediately.

Raise your hand and call for help loudly
🔒

Lost Child

Report immediately to the nearest WOW staff member. A child-safe lost-and-found protocol is activated instantly. The park announcement system is used if required.

Go to any staff in red WOW uniform
🚒

General Emergency

WOW has on-site security and an emergency response plan. For any serious emergency, alert the nearest staff member or security post immediately.

National Emergency: 112
